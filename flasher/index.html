4 Flash Firmware

The nRF52840 uses a UF2 bootloader. Switch the device into DFU mode and it appears as a USB drive — drop the .uf2 file onto it to flash instantly.

🛡️ Safe flashing: The bootloader cannot be overwritten. An interrupted transfer leaves the previous firmware intact. Your device cannot be bricked.
